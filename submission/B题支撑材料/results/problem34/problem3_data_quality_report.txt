Problem 3 Data Quality Report
=============================
Data sources:
- 2025 Zhejiang Statistical Yearbook tables (2024 data):
   - 17-25: main indicators (population, GDP, fiscal)
   - 17-26: year-end population (urban/rural split)
   - 17-34: passenger and freight volume
   - 17-35: highway mileage, expressway, auto count
   - 17-42: culture and health (sports venues, hospitals)
- data/raw/problem3_missing_fields_lookup.csv: manually collected
  rail station, airport and representative venue fields.
- Prefecture-city values aggregated from constituent district rows.
- Coordinates: manually curated administrative-center coordinates.

Quality scope:
- Core statistical indicators are quality grade A because they come from
  official yearbook tables.
- Rail station, airport, stadium name and stadium capacity fields are
  auxiliary manual lookup fields. Their row-level status is encoded as
  A_core; aux_verified, A_core; aux_partial or A_core; aux_verified(在建).

Known data gaps:
- Urban rail transit (metro) passenger data is missing for Hangzhou, Ningbo,
  and Shaoxing in the yearbook because the district-level table does not
  separately report metro system statistics. This may understate transport_score
  for these cities. Paper should note this limitation.
- Blank expressway_km, passenger_volume_10k, rail_transit_passenger_10k
  and auto_count values are treated as 0 in the transport composite. For
  rail_transit_passenger_10k, this only means the yearbook cell is blank;
  it is not interpreted as no passenger rail station.
- Stadium capacity is still blank for 10 regions: 云和县、仙居县、岱山县、嵊泗县、常山县、平湖市、松阳县、缙云县、象山县、龙游县.
- Stadium name is still blank for 1 regions: 缙云县.
- Some auxiliary venue records are partial or under construction and should
  not be treated as official completed venue-capacity data.

Score construction:
- population_score / gdp_score: log-minmax normalization of raw values.
- transport_score: composite of log(road_km), log(expressway_km),
  log(passenger_volume), log(rail_passenger), log(auto_count), then minmax.
- football_score: composite of absolute venue count (70%) and venue density
  per 10k population (30%), then log-minmax normalization. It is a public
  sports-atmosphere proxy, not a football-only statistic.
- sports_base_score: log-minmax normalization of broad sports venue count;
  this is interpreted as public sports infrastructure, not match-ready stadiums.
- capacity_score: entropy-weighted composite of sports-base,
  stadium-capacity, rail-access and airport-access scores.
- Missing stadium capacity or stadium name receives the lowest capacity tier.
- partial auxiliary venue records multiply the stadium-capacity subscore by
  0.85; in-progress venue records multiply that subscore by 0.75.
- Rail access is 0 for no passenger rail, 60 for nearest/limited service,
  and 100 for explicit local passenger rail stations.
- Airport access follows the conservative distance bands documented in
  docs/problem34/problem3_model_notes.md.

Auxiliary quality flags:
- partial regions (19): 三门县、云和县、仙居县、兰溪市、天台县、岱山县、嵊泗县、常山县、平湖市、庆元县、景宁畲族自治县、松阳县、永康市、江山市、磐安县、绍兴市、缙云县、遂昌县、龙游县.
- in-progress venue regions (4): 余姚市、平阳县、开化县、武义县.

Use in paper:
- Cite "2025年浙江统计年鉴" as primary data source.
- Acknowledge that metro data is missing for Hangzhou/Ningbo/Shaoxing.
- Describe rail, airport and representative venue fields as manually
  collected public-information supplements, not official yearbook indicators.
- Concrete venue capacity is now used in capacity_score, but blank/partial/
  in-progress records are conservatively downweighted.
