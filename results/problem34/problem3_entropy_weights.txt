Entropy Weight Method — Computed Weights
========================================

Weights are derived via the Entropy Weight Method (熵权法).
For each indicator group, a normalised decision matrix is built from all
64 regions (sub-indicator level) or all 7,680 pair-venue combinations
(objective level). The entropy of each column is computed, and the
information utility d_j = 1 - e_j determines the final weight.

Interpretation:
- Higher weight → the indicator varies more across alternatives, carrying
  more discriminative information.
- Lower weight → the indicator is more uniform across alternatives and
  contributes less to differentiating good venues from poor ones.
